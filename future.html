Future Research

Further research is essential to better understand the impact of public networks and keep everyday users safe from network threats. Future studies should focus on improving protection tools such as firewalls, encryption, and network monitoring. Another research opportunity is examining how people interact with public networks. Exploring the long‑term effects of public Wi‑Fi access would also provide crucial information to improve productivity, privacy, and digital inclusion.
