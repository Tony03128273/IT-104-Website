Potential Benefits

Some potential benefits of public Wi‑Fi are convenience, productivity, and accessibility. Wi‑Fi provides a free and easy way to access the Internet in everyday places such as a coffee shop, libraries, or an airport. Another advantage is that it helps students and members of the workforce complete work while away from home. Lastly, Wi‑Fi offers an Internet connection to individuals who may not have service at home or a reliable connection.

Quick Examples

  • Coffee shops and libraries enabling research and study on the go.
  • Airports supporting mobile work while traveling.
  • Students accessing class materials when away from campus housing.
