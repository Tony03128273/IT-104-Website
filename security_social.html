Security & Social Concerns

Public Wi‑Fi presents important security and social concerns. Unsecured hotspots make it easier for attackers to intercept passwords, private messages, or financial details. Because people often assume the network will keep them safe, they may overshare without realizing the risks. Awareness and a few simple protections go a long way toward keeping your data out of the wrong hands.
