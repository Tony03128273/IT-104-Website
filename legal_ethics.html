Legal & Ethical Issues

Although public Wi‑Fi offers many benefits, it also presents significant legal and ethical issues. Some legal issues include transmitting malware across the network, accessing restricted websites, or sharing copyrighted materials. Ethical concerns include users connecting to a network without knowing it is unsecured, resulting in their information being stolen. A business that provides public Wi‑Fi also has a responsibility to protect its network and inform customers of potential threats.
